Our Story

Born in Brickfields,
Loved Across Malaysia

Seni Sattisorru began as a beloved institution in Brickfields — Kuala Lumpur's vibrant "Little India" — and has since grown into a nationwide culinary landmark. Our Klang branch brings the same authentic flavours, the same clay pots, and the same passion right to your neighbourhood.

Every dish is crafted with the finest herbs and spices, slow-cooked to perfection in handcrafted clay pots that seal in flavour and aroma. From the first bite of our tender Sattisorru to the last spoonful of our fragrant Mutton Soup — you taste the tradition.
