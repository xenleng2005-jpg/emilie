Our Story

Klang's Beloved
Western Kitchen

Established in 1999, Restoran Windmill has been a cornerstone of Bandar Baru Klang's dining scene for over 25 years. Easily recognised by the iconic large windmill at our shopfront, we've built a legacy of serving hearty Western comfort food with a local twist.

From sizzling lamb chops and tender ribeye steaks to local Asian favourites — every dish is crafted with passion and prepared fresh daily. Whether you're here for a family dinner, a romantic candlelit meal, or a quick weekday lunch, Windmill is always your home away from home.

We are proud to be fully Halal certified, welcoming guests of all backgrounds to share in our love of great food.
