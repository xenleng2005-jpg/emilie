Our Story

Authentic South Indian
Claypot Experience

Welcome to Seni Sattisorru Klang Branch — where tradition meets flavour. "Satti Sorru" means claypot rice in Tamil, and we have perfected this ancient cooking method to deliver an unmatched dining experience.

Every dish is freshly cooked in a traditional clay pot, infused with aromatic herbs and hand-ground spices. From our piping hot claypot sets to our legendary Thiruvila Saapadu banana leaf feast — every bite is a celebration of South Indian heritage.
