Our Story

A Hidden Beast
Awakened in PJ

Tucked beneath a massage spa in Aman Suria, The Gathering Beast is Petaling Jaya's most exciting culinary secret. What appears unassuming from the outside transforms into an extraordinary dining experience — a chic, warmly-lit space where Chinese tradition meets bold, modern imagination.

Our chefs breathe new life into beloved Malaysian Chinese flavours, crafting dishes that honour their roots while daring to surprise. From 24-hour slow-cooked beef tongue to golden crispy kuey teow in triple-fish broth, every plate is a conversation.

Round tables with lazy Susans, antique earthenware, calligraphy on brick walls, and an open kitchen — this is where the table becomes the gathering point for unforgettable moments.

Culinary Creations

The Menu

Bold, creative, and deeply rooted in Chinese culinary tradition — signature dishes that have become legends among regulars.

01
🥩
Signature

Beef Tongue Steak

Slow-cooked at 75°C for a full 24 hours, glazed with Huangjiu rice wine, garnished with pickled nangka and sengkuang. Meltingly tender, deeply complex.

02
🫕
Signature

Three Kingdom Beef

An offal lover's triumph — silken tripe and tendon with celeriac and roasted cashews, dressed in a bold balsamic vinegar reduction.

03
🍜
Chef's Pick

Golden Crispy Fried Kuey Teow

Lacquered crispy kuey teow submerged in a rich, steamy broth built from three varieties of fish, crowned with fresh crab meat and sliced snapper.

04
🍗
Fan Favourite

Dry-Aged Chicken & Quail

Expertly dry-aged poultry showcasing the beauty of patience and precision in Chinese cooking — exceptional depth of flavour in every bite.

Must-Try

Fermented Pork Balls

Delicately fermented pork served with a luscious creamy century egg sauce that brings nostalgia and novelty together in each mouthful.

Grilled Argentinian Squid

Premium Argentinian squid expertly grilled over high heat for the perfect char — tender, smoky, elegantly presented with the chef's secret accompaniments.

Paella Chicken Rice

A bold East-meets-West creation — Chinese chicken rice reimagined with Spanish paella technique, producing a socarrat-base rice of stunning complexity.
