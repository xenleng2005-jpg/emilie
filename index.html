Our Story

Born in Brickfields,
Now in Your Neighbourhood

Senivasagam Manikam — known as "Seni" — spent nearly 10 years perfecting his base recipe and cooking method before opening the original Seni Sattisorru in Brickfields, Kuala Lumpur. Today, Klang gets to enjoy the same legendary flavours.

What sets Seni Sattisorru apart is the method: onions, tomatoes, curry leaves and masala are sautéed first, then layered with your chosen protein and cooked rice in a traditional clay pot — creating a dish that is fragrant, robust and utterly unique.
