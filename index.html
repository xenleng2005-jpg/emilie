Our Story · 我们的故事

Authentic Flavours,
Every Single Bowl

Yoo Noodle (优一麵館) brings the warmth of traditional Malaysian Chinese noodle craftsmanship to Kota Damansara. We believe every bowl should tell a story — crafted with handmade noodles, rich broth, and the finest ingredients.

From silky thin strands to hearty pinched pieces, choose your noodle style. Whether you crave a steaming soup or a bold dry toss, we have the perfect dish waiting for you.
