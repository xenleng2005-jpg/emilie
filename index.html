Our Story

Where Tradition
Meets the Wild

Hidden beneath a massage spa in Aman Suria, The Gathering Beast is a culinary adventure unlike any other in Petaling Jaya. A sweeping exposed-brick facade, towering glass windows, and an abundance of potted plants make it look more like a chic downtown darling than a suburban gem.

Step inside to find antique earthenware, ornate figurines, calligraphy on the walls, and round tables with Lazy Susans — exuding deep Chinese tradition. The open kitchen pulses with energy while our chefs craft modern magic from ancient Malaysian Chinese flavours.

🔥
Precision cooking — 75°C sous-vide, 24-hour slow cooks, and triple-cooked techniques
🏛️
Chinese antique décor with a vibrant modern open-kitchen concept
🥩
Renowned for bold bovine creations and daring creative fusion fare
🌿
Fresh, locally-sourced ingredients elevated with international flair
