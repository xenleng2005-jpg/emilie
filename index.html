Our Story

The Heart of Malaysian Flavour

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Bukit Bintang, Kuala Lumpur, Cakchibo House is a celebration of authentic Malaysian flavours — where every dish tells a story passed down through generations.

From the fragrant coconut-laced Nasi Lemak to the boldly spiced Rendang Tok and the hearty Patin Tempoyak, our kitchen honours traditional recipes while serving them in a warm, modern setting.

Rendang Tok —
Perak Heritage

Our Rendang Tok is cooked low and slow for over 4 hours, allowing the coconut milk to fully caramelise and coat every tender piece of beef. The secret lies in the blend of fresh galangal, lemongrass, toasted kerisik, and our chef's private spice paste.

The dish that regulars come back for again and again — dark, rich, deeply fragrant, and utterly unforgettable.
