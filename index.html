Our Story

A Klang Institution
Since 1999

Restoran Windmill has been a beloved landmark in Bandar Baru Klang since 1999. What started as a classic Western grill has grown into a full dining experience — blending bold sizzling favourites with Thai-Asian comfort food.

Located at 18–20, Lorong Tiara 1A, we serve generous portions of ribeye steak, aromatic lamb chops, tiger prawns, creamy pastas and more — all at accessible prices that keep families coming back.

With a 4.4 ★ rating from over 700 diners, our kitchen has earned its place as the go-to sizzling house of Klang. Fully Halal certified.
